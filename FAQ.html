FAQ! Need Help?

1. Does the price include recurring scrapes?
No, the price is for creating a scraping script and running it once. We offer fully managed solutions at a price of $99/month per website. You can specify how often you want the data to be scrapped.
2. Is there any upfront cost?
No, there are no hidden upfront costs. The pricing discussed is what you pay.
3. What format the data will be delivered?
We can deliver data in various formats including CSV, JSON, XML, or directly into your database.
4. How soon can I get the data?
Turnaround time depends on the complexity of the website, but standard scrapes are usually delivered within 24-48 hours.
5. What information do I need to provide?
Simply provide the target website URL and list the specific data fields you need extracted.
